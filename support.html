Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping typically takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping options are available at checkout for faster delivery.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all products in their original condition. Custom orders are non-refundable.

Do you offer assembly services?

Most of our stools come fully assembled. For products requiring assembly, detailed instructions are included. Professional assembly services are available in select areas.

Can I customize my order?

Yes! We offer customization options for materials, finishes, and dimensions. Contact us via WhatsApp to discuss your custom requirements.

What materials are used in your products?

We use sustainable materials including reclaimed wood, eco-friendly metals, and premium fabrics. Each product page lists specific materials used.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we ship within the United States. International shipping options are coming soon.
